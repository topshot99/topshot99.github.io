# About Me

## My Journey

I love diving deep into technologies, understanding how they work under the hood, and sharing my discoveries with the community. My work often involves:

- **Database Technologies**: Particularly Azure Cosmos DB, NoSQL patterns, and data optimization
- **Cloud Computing**: Azure services, serverless architectures, and scalability challenges  
- **Performance Engineering**: Identifying bottlenecks, optimizing queries, and improving system efficiency

## Why This Blog?

This blog serves as my digital notebook where I document:
